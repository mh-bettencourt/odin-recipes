Creamy Apple Oatmeal

Description

A creamy oatmeal recipe, combined with apple and cinnamon, the classic combo.

This is perfect for breakfast or a snack, made in advance for the whole week.

Keeps well in the refrigerator up to 5 days.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium apples, chopped
  • 1 cup coarse oats
  • 1 cup fine oats
  • 1,3qt of water
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. Place the apples and a bit of water in a 2qt sauce pan
  2. Saute the apples until tender, in medium heat
  3. Add the remaining water and cinnamon
  4. Reduce to low heat
  5. Add the oats, stirring frequently
  6. Cook for 5 min, or until the coarse oats are tender
  7. When done, place the oatmeal in individual bowls
  8. Serve with your favorite toppings
