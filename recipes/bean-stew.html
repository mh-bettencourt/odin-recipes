Pinto Bean Stew

Description

A flavorful and filling bean stew, best accompanied by a side of brown rice and a fresh salad.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 cups dried pinto beans
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 medium tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 large bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 5 dried allspice berries
  • 5 dried cloves
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp regular paprika
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp tumeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp coarse sea salt

Steps

  1. Soak the beans overnight
  2. Rinse under fresh running water
  3. Place all the ingredients in a 6qt electric pressure cooker
  4. Stir and cover with the lid
  5. Turn on the pressure cooker, select the Bean program and adjust time to 31 minutes
  6. Follow manufacturer's instructions for a natural pressure release
  7. When the pressure valve has dropped, remove the lid and stir
  8. Serve while warm
