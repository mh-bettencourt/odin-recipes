Hearty Soup

Description

This is a thick soup, filled with chunky vegetables and chickpeas, quickly made in a 6qt electric pressure cooker.

It's very satisfying by itself or as a side for baked potatoes, making it a perfect warming meal on cold days.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chickpeas
  • 8 cups of water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 8 carrots, chopped
  • 6 cauliflower florets, chopped
  • 2 white cabbages, chopped
  • 1 zucchini,chopped
  • 1 orange sweet potato, chopped
  • 1 yellow sweet potato, chopped

Steps

  1. Place the chickpeas at the bottom of the pot
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and cover with the lid
  3. Turn on the pressure cooker, select the Soup program and adjust time to 2 minutes
  4. Follow manufacturer's instructions for a quick pressure release and remove the lid
  5. With an immersion blender, partially bend the soup until the desired texture is achieved
  6. Serve while warm
